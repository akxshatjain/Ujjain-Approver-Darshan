The Legend

Shri Mahakaleshwar is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas, the only one facing south (Dakshinamukhi). It is believed that Lord Shiva resides here in the form of Mahakal, the ruler of time and death.

Darshan Timings

  • Morning Darshan 7:00 AM - 1:00 PM
  • Evening Aarti 6:30 PM - 7:00 PM
  • Shayan Aarti 11:00 PM
